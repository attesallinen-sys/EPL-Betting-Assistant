# EPL Context -- 2025-26 Season

Reference information about the English Premier League to help the agent contextualize predictions and write informed analysis.

## League Format

- **Competition**: English Premier League (EPL), the top tier of English football
- **Teams**: 20 clubs compete each season
- **Matches**: 38 matchweeks (gameweeks), each team plays every other team home and away (380 total matches)
- **Season span**: August 16, 2025 -- May 24, 2026
- **Match days**: Primarily Saturday (12:30, 15:00, 17:30 UK time), with some fixtures on Friday evening, Sunday, and Monday
- **Points**: Win = 3 points, Draw = 1 point, Loss = 0 points
- **Relegation**: Bottom 3 teams are relegated to the Championship
- **European qualification**: Top finishers qualify for UEFA Champions League, Europa League, and Conference League

## Key Context for 2025-26

- **Defending champions**: Liverpool won the 2024-25 title by a 10-point margin.
- **Promoted teams**: Leeds United, Burnley, and Sunderland were promoted from the Championship. Promoted teams historically face tough adaptation; survival is their primary objective.
- **Relegated from 2024-25**: Leicester City, Ipswich Town, Southampton.
- **Technology**: First full season with semi-automated offside technology (SAOT).

## Key Terms for Predictions

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| 1X2 | Match result betting: 1 = Home win, X = Draw, 2 = Away win |
| Over/Under 2.5 | Whether total goals in the match will be over or under 2.5 |
| BTTS | Both Teams To Score -- whether both teams will score at least once |
| Clean Sheet | A team conceding zero goals in a match |
| Form | A team's recent results, typically last 5-6 matches |
| H2H | Head-to-head -- historical record between two specific teams |
| xG | Expected Goals -- a statistical measure of chance quality |
| GD | Goal Difference -- goals scored minus goals conceded |
| Correct Score | Predicting the exact final scoreline |
| Double Chance | Betting on two of three outcomes (e.g., Home or Draw) |
| Handicap | Adjusted result with a virtual goal advantage/disadvantage |

## Factors That Influence Match Outcomes

When synthesizing reasoning across prediction sources, these are the main factors to consider:

- **Current form**: Recent results and performance trends
- **Home/away record**: Some teams perform significantly differently at home vs. away
- **Head-to-head history**: Historical results between the two teams
- **Injuries and suspensions**: Key player absences can shift predictions
- **Squad rotation**: Teams in European competition or cup runs may rotate players
- **Managerial changes**: New managers can cause short-term volatility in results
- **Promoted team adjustments**: Newly promoted teams often struggle early in the season
- **Fixture congestion**: Teams with midweek matches may fatigue
- **Motivation**: Title race, relegation battle, European qualification, or derby significance
